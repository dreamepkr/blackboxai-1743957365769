About Kshetri Community

The Kshetri community, also known as Chhetri, is one of the largest ethnic groups in Nepal, known for their rich cultural heritage and significant contributions to Nepali society.

Our community values include bravery, honesty, and strong family bonds, which have been passed down through generations.

This platform serves as a hub for Kshetri people worldwide to connect, share knowledge, and preserve our traditions.

Quick Facts

  • One of Nepal's largest ethnic groups
  • Traditional occupation: Military and agriculture
  • Major festivals: Dashain, Tihar, Maghe Sankranti
  • Distinct cultural dress: Daura Suruwal for men, Gunyu Cholo for women
